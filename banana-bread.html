Banana Bread

Prep: 5 minutes

Bake: 60 minutes

Oven: 350F

Makes: 1 loaf

2all-purpose flour
1 teaspoonbaking soda
1/4 teaspoonsalt
2eggs
1/2 cupbutter, softened
3/4 cuppacked brown sugar
2 1/3 cupsbananas
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Grease a 9x5" loaf pan.
  3. Combine flour, baking soda, salt in a large bowl.
  4. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  5. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined.
  6. Bake at 350 until a toothpick inserted into the centre of the loaf comes out clean.
  7. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to Cool completely.
